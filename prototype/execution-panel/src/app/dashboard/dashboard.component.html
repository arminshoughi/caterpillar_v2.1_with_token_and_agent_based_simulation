A Model-Driven Development Platform for Generating and Simulating Decentralized Applications

  • Context: The emergence of blockchain technologyhas facilitated innovative solutions for various domains, such assupply chains, healthcare, and social networking. Decentralizedapplication development can be challenging for citizen devel-opers without having technical knowledge. Even for the moreexperienced smart contract developer, decentralized applications’complexity can significantly delay the development process.

  • Method: Model-Driven Development platforms shift the focusof software development activity from coding to modeling forsoftware development companies. Accordingly, in this study, weintroduce a model-driven development platform for generatingsmart contract-based decentralized applications according totheir business models. The proposed platform enables citizendevelopers to design, deploy and simulate the behavior of de-centralized applications on actual permissioned or permissionlessblockchain systems.

  • Results: We evaluate the feasibility, usefulness, and efficiency ofthe platform in generating decentralized applications. The resultsshow that the proposed platform. . . provides a reliable approach for creatingmodel-driven development platforms for distributed hyperledgertechnology.
